RULES AND DISCIPLINE FOR ADMISSION IN THE BABUS HOSTELS
  1. Hostel Timing – Hostel will be kept opened from 6.00 a.m to 9.00 p.m. All the tenants are requested to return to the Hostel within the stipulated time.
  2. Admission to the Hostel will be cancelled if false information is furnished.
  3. Tenants are required to give their consent about continuing the Hostel Facilities when asked by the Hostel Management.
  4. The Babus Hostels reserves its right to cancel admission of undeserving students without giving any reason.
  5. The Babus Hostels will not be responsible for any mishap.
  6. The Babus Hostels reserves the right to increase the Hostel fee, if necessary.
  7. Tenants residing in The Babus Hostels shall strictly observe all the rules and regulations in force from time to time. Breach of rules / regulations may invite termination / fine.
  8. During their stay in the hostel, tenants will be under the control of the officials of The Babus Hostels.
  9. Smoking, consumption of alcoholic drinks, prohibited videos, drugs or any other intoxicants are strictly prohibited in the rooms, hostel premises. Any violation of this will result into termination and forfeiture of security deposit.
  10. No guest or visitor of the tenants will be permitted to visit rooms. Visitors and guests should be received in the reception area only.
  11. Every tenant shall be in her or his room by 9.00 pm. if he or she has to stay out after the said timing owing to any special reason, he or she must obtain prior permission from the admin. The application for leave of absence should be submitted.
  12. No Tenants shall use the service of Hostel servant for personal work even on payment. He shall also not bring any servant from outside even temporarily.
  13. Tenants should not enter rooms of other students without permission of inmates. Tenants should not go to other tenants room after 11.00 pm.
  14. Every case of illness and accident must be reported immediately to the warden in charge.
  15. No posters, wall hangings, wall writings etc., should be put up anywhere, either in rooms or lobbies.
  16. Tenants are not allowed to play any kind of sports in the rooms.
  17. Throwing of water, color etc. on one another walls / property of the hostel is strictly prohibited. Any such action will attract penalty and fine and the tenant must repair at their cost.
  18. The monthly rent should be paid in advance to the management on or before 30th of every month. Any delay will be treated as intentions to terminate the contract and entry to the rooms will be prohibited.
  19. Solid items, wastes and garbages should be thrown into the dust bin separately provided and it should not be flushed in the western closet toilet.
  20. Any tenant going out on holidays should inform in written, the date, time, purpose etc. to the Admin and enter in the register, separately maintained in the office.
  21. The Babus Hostels is not responsible for lose of any personal belongings. It is strictly Tenants responsibility in safeguarding their belongings such as cash, jewelry, mobile, laptops etc.,
  22. Tenants are expected to switch off their lights and fans in their rooms every time they go out and take precautions to minimize their electricity consumption.
  23. Charges for any damage to the property as well as to the furniture, fixtures, amenities, electrical items, fittings etc., caused by tenants / tenants negligence will be recovered from the tenant / tenants staying in the said room / floor.
  24. Hostel is meant only for the use of the bonafide tenant.
  25. No music system is allowed in the Hostel
  26. Before leaving the hostel, the tenant must pay all the dues, handover the charges of the room and other material in satisfactory condition to the Admin of The Babus Hostels.
  27. Allotment of room, furniture, amenities and fixtures will be at the discretion of the Management of The Babus Hostels and no complaint in this regard will be entertained.
  28. Tenant must keep the room allotted to him in clean and neat condition. He shall take proper care of the furniture, fixtures, electronic items, fittings in the room. The management have the right to enter and inspect the rooms at any time, even in the absence of the students.
  29. All matters relating to the differences among tenants and complaints about the hostel servants shall be brought to the notice of the management of the hostel, whom will take such action as may be necessary. No police complaint will be lodged by tenants before taking prior permission from the management.
  30. If vacating in between the agreed period, the security deposit will not be refunded
  31. Any complaints, indecent behavior, noisy, creating problems to the neighbor rooms and disturbing the neighbors unnecessarily is strictly prohibited and any such action will result into termination of the tenancy agreement.
  32. Tenants suffering from any contagious diseases will not be allowed to stay in the hostel due to health issues for other occupants.
  33. One month notice should be given, prior to leaving the hostel. Otherwise, 30 days rent & amenities fees will be charged and shall be deducted from the caution deposit.
  34. If the Management finds any banned items in the rooms such as firearms, weapons, electric heaters, stoves, etc., the same will be ceased and it will not return to the tenants.
  35. Candles and incense are fire hazardous and are not permitted in the hostels. Combustible materials such as gasoline, paint thinner and oil lamps are not permitted in the hostels. Burning/bursting of crackers, carrying of crackers to the rooms and lighting of lamps/candles are banned strictly in and around the Hostel premises. Any accidents incurred by the inmates due to the banned items will be their responsibility and all the related expenses should be met by the tenants.
  36. If one room gets vacated by one of the occupants, the rest of the occupants need to get adjusted in another room for the purpose of economy of expenses. If the other wants to remain alone, he will have to pay the rental of single occupancy and the actual electrical charges incurred for the room.
  37. It is compulsory all the tenants must punch in their entry and exit every time in the attendance machine kept in the entry. If not followed tenants will be asked to vacate.
